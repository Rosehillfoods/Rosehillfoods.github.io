Job title: Sauce & Marinade Cook

Service: Liquid Sauce Preparation & Packaging

Position reports to: Production Supervisor

Date: Immediately available

 

Rose Hill Foods, a company (SQF and HACCP certified) established in the preparation of dry soups, sauces, seasonings, liquid sauces and marinades, is actively looking for a talented, dedicated and self-motivated individual to manufacture and package sauces and marinades.

 

1.    SUMMARY OF POSITION

The Sauce & Marinade Cook will be responsible for liquid sauce preparation and packaging in a food processing plant, following strict preparation and packaging specifications.

 

2.    TASKS & RESPONSIBILITIES

Main tasks and responsibilities

     Prepares and regulates kettles to blend and cook sauces.

     Prepares and selects ingredients before starting production.

     Regulates temperatures and blending speed of various sauce preparation.

     Must follow formulations and process.

     Has sample approved by QA team.

     Empties (discharges) finished product to requested packaging format.

     Labels packed product as per specifications.

     Packages the finished product (sauces & marinades) in jars and bags.

     Washes all kettles and equipment in production area.

 

3.    QUALIFICATIONS

Language

     Preferably bilingual French & English, spoken and written.

Education/experience

     2 years of experience in a similar position; OR

     Culinary School graduate

Professional skills

     Excellent attendance and reliability.

     Willingness to work overtime when needed.

     Safety first attitude.

     Good physical condition with ability to lift up to 25 Kg occasionally.
